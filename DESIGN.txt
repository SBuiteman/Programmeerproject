- API: - wger.de, database containing exercises inclucing instructions and pictures.
       - SQLiteOpenHelper, allows to block long running database operations to not
	 be run at startup of the app.
- Datasource: SQL, storing and retreiving data produced by user.

- Database: 
	- Progress table: containing weight, reps and sets per exercise and how many
	  times the exercise was performed.
	- Workouts table: containing all the included exercises and their settings per workout.

	-Exercises and workouts will be strings an the rest integers.
